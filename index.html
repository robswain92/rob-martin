Rob Martin

Guitar-Vocalist from the East Midlands

Based in the East Midlands, Rob Martin is a guitar-vocalist with over 30 years of professional experience.


Having honed his skills on the pub and club circuits, Rob covers an extensive range of hits from the 1960s through to the 1990s.

Rob Martin provides complete daytime and night-time entertainment and is available for pubs, clubs, private functions, weddings and just about any other sort of occasion you could imagine!
